LabInsight Pro: Bridging Health Literacy Gap in Nigeria.

LabInsight Pro is an educational tool designed to improve health literacy by helping users understand common laboratory tests. Every year, millions of Nigerians receive lab test results with little or no explanation. This creates confusion, anxiety, and misunderstanding before they return to their doctor.

LabInsight Pro solves this problem by providing simple, clear and accessible explanations of lab tests, written in a language anyone can understand. The goal is not to diagnose or replace doctors, but to bridge the knowledge gap between lab test results and professional medical consultation.

This project was created with a focus on:

Clarity: Breaking down medical terms into everyday language.

Safety: Providing strictly non-diagnostic, educational information.

Impact: Supporting patients in making sense of their health journey.

Accessibility: Fast to use, mobile-friendly and Nigerian-context aware.

LabInsight Pro is built as a student project for the GESP Yabatech Hackathon 2026, with a mission to promote health awareness and reduce the fear that often comes with receiving medical results.

LabInsight Pro is a health-tech platform that helps users understand laboratory test results through clear, AI-assisted explanations.
